Responsible Gaming

If you or someone you know has a gambling addiction and wants help please call 1-800-Gambler.
Gambling is intended to be a fun hobby and our site tries to provide betters with tools to make it more rewarding and fun. But player safety is and will always be our number one concern.

Even though for most gambling is simply a pasttime that helps to spice up sporting events, we acknowledge there are some users who may get carried away or become addicted to betting. For those players or friends of those players please reach out to 1-800-Gambler or any other organization that helps overcome gambling addictions.
Please help us keep the sport of sportsbetting fun, and most importantly safe.
